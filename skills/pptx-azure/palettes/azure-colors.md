## Usage Tips

- Use Azure Blue (`#0078D4`) as the dominant accent color
- Pair with neutrals for professional look
- Reserve bright colors (Success, Warning, Error) for semantic meaning
- Maintain 4.5:1 contrast ratio for accessibility
